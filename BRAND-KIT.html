07 — Usage Rules
Do's & Don'ts
✓
Maintain clear space
Keep a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "W" mark around all sides of the logo.
✓
Use approved colors
Only use the logo in the color combinations shown in this guide. Navy on light, white on dark.
✓
Scale proportionally
Always scale the logo uniformly. The minimum size for the full lockup is 120px wide.
✕
Don't distort
Never stretch, compress, rotate, or skew the logo. Keep the original proportions at all times.
✕
Don't recolor
Don't apply gradients, shadows, outlines, or unapproved color combinations to the logo.
✕
Don't separate the dot
The ".ai" is integral to the brand. Never separate "wirig" from ".ai" or change their relative sizing.
